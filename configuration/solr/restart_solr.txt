
Motivation
----------
1. $ cd $SOLR_HOME
2. $ bin/solr restart
3. The above command may give something similar to ->  Solr home directory /home/saketh/Software/solr/solr-6.5.1 must contain a solr.xml file!

Configuration
-------------
1. $ cd $SOLR_HOME
2. $ cp ./server/solr/solr.xml .

Result
------
Now you can restart Solr by
1. $ cd $SOLR_HOME
2. $ bin/solr restart
